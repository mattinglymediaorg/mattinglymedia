THE ATHLETE
BEHIND THE BRAND

Amber Anning is a world champion track & field athlete — one of the most decorated sprinters of her generation. On the track she competes at the highest level. Off it, she's building something just as powerful: a personal brand that reflects who she actually is.

Her story deserves to be told with the same precision and intensity she brings to her sport. That's where I come in.

My Role

BRAND IDENTITY

Building a visual identity that goes beyond athletics — capturing her personality, values, and story in a way that resonates with fans, sponsors, and media.

CONTENT CREATION

Producing consistent, high-quality video content designed for social media — training footage, event coverage, behind-the-scenes moments — all edited to keep her audience engaged.

ENGAGEMENT GROWTH

Creating content that doesn't just look good — it performs. Every piece is crafted to grow her following, increase watch time, and build genuine connections with her audience.

"A world champion's brand should be built to the same standard as their performance. That's the bar I hold myself to on every piece of content I create for Amber."

— Ty Mattingly, Mattingly Media
